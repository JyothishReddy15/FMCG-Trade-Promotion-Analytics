# FMCG Trade Promotion Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes FMCG trade promotions using Power BI to evaluate sales performance, inventory levels, and promotion effectiveness across different regions and product categories.

---

## Business Objective

- Measure promotion effectiveness
- Analyze regional sales performance
- Monitor inventory trends
- Identify top-performing products
- Support business decision-making

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Power Query

---

## Dataset

The project contains:

- Product Master
- Region Master
- Promotion Master
- POS Sales
- Inventory
- Distributor Sales
- Calendar

---

## Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI Cards
- Weekly Sales Trend
- Sales vs Inventory Trend
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Product Category
- Promotion vs Non-Promotion Analysis
- Top 6 Products
- Interactive Slicers

---

## KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Quantity
- Total Inventory
- Total Shipments
- Promotion Effectiveness
